Laying the Foundation for Lifelong Learning

At Saud International School, our Early Learning Stage is more than just a beginning ‚Äî it is where a child's journey into curiosity, confidence, and character truly takes root.
Spanning from KG3 to Reception 2, this foundational phase is intentionally designed to support a child's development at every stage ‚Äî intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically ‚Äî within a nurturing and enriched learning environment.

Curriculum Framework: A Holistic, Values-Driven Approach

Our curriculum draws inspiration from international early years best practices, and is aligned with developmental milestones, language acquisition frameworks, and age-appropriate academic readiness. We integrate elements from global standards while remaining sensitive to cultural identity and individual growth.
Through play-based learning, structured activities, and intentional teaching moments, the Early Learning Stage nurtures early literacy, numeracy, communication, independence, emotional resilience, and a love for learning.

Across all levels of the Early Learning Stage, students engage in a rich program that includes:

  • English Language Development (Phonics, Vocabulary, Speaking & Listening)

  • Mathematics (Counting, Shapes, Measurement, Patterns, Logic)

  • Thematic Science & Discovery

  • Fine and Gross Motor Skills (PE, Music, Movement)

  • Art & Creativity

  • Arabic Language

  • Islamic Studies (age-appropriate and story-based for Muslim students)

  • Personal, Social, and Emotional Development (PSED)

  • Technology & Interactive Learning Tools (age-appropriate screen-free integration)
